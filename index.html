ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Yashraj Munda

a student pursuing my degree at St. Xavier's College, Ranchi. While studying, I also work as a freelance creative, specializing in UI/UX design, web development, and photo and video editing. Passionate about building brands, I help clients boost their online presence by managing content and social media accounts on platforms like YouTube and Instagram. My services range from logo and poster design to video editing and strategic content management—all aimed at creating memorable digital experiences. I'm the creator of the YouTube channel "YashRaj Munda," where I share informative content reflecting my interests and expertise. Beyond work, I'm an avid gamer—both online and offline—and enjoy playing football and badminton. I'm also a proud gold medalist in taekwondo.
